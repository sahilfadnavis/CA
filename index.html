Responsibilities

Promotion

As a campus ambassador, your primary role is to generate awareness and excitement about the technical fest. This involves using various marketing techniques, both online and offline, to ensure that the event is well-publicized and that students on campus are informed about the fest's details, schedule, and offerings.

Recruitment

You'll be responsible for encouraging students to participate in the technical fest. This includes reaching out to potential participants, forming teams, and recruiting volunteers who can help with the fest's organization and execution. Your role is to get as many students involved as possible.

Event Coordination

To ensure the fest runs smoothly, you'll assist in planning and organizing various events, workshops, and competitions. This involves coordinating schedules, setting up venues, and ensuring that all activities proceed as planned to provide a positive experience for attendees.

Content Creation

Creating content is crucial to building excitement and anticipation for the technical fest. You'll generate blog posts, videos, social media updates, and other engaging content to showcase the fest's unique technical challenges, innovations, and activities.

Feedback Collection

Gathering feedback is essential for continuous improvement. You'll collect input from participants and attendees to understand what went well and what can be enhanced for future editions of the technical fest. This feedback is valuable for the fest's organizers

Logistics and Support

In addition to promoting and recruiting, you'll assist in the practical aspects of the fest. This includes helping with logistics, such as setting up venues, ensuring equipment is ready, and providing technical support to participants during competitions and challenges. Your role is to facilitate a seamless experience for everyone involved.
